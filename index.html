"Developing Software

White Paper on Software Development

"Now, designing software is important for the simple reason that it lowers the costs of changing plans in the middle of the building process. Individuals may all of a sudden change their minds, but the design process can also bring to light the needs for changes rather than they be found during development. In addition, it allows individuals from different teams that speak different languages to discuss the objectives, inner-workings, and expectations of the software amongst themselves and with the client. In this document it is outlined how to design software in a way that ensures maintainability, scalability, and reliability. These are three concerns of software that should be addressed because doing so ensures the overall health and profit of the developed software."
